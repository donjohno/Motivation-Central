How to get your beauty rest

Why is rest important?

A study conducted by Charles King who is an MBA student at the University of Stellenbosch (USB) stated that:

“Lack of sleep is not only related to workplace issues such as absenteeism, lack of productivity, poor work performance, and accidents – which have a direct cost impact on a business – but insufficient sleep has been directly linked with seven of the 15 leading causes of death.. The cost to just one medical aid scheme of treating life-threatening diseases linked to sleeping less than the recommended eight hours a night is estimated at R22bn annually".

Tips on how to fall asleep

1. Start a new sleeping cycle
In theory, resetting your sleeping schedule is the most difficult part of creating a new routine, because our routines and habits are powerful. So you can anticipate that you won't fall asleep when starting your new sleeping routine. Therefore you need to prepare yourself for bed hours ahead.
2. Avoid caffine after 3pm
One study examined the effects of caffeine intake zero, three, and six hours before bedtime, and found that even caffeine consumed six hours before bed time could reduce sleep time by one hour.


3. Avoid screen time at least 1 hour before bed time
